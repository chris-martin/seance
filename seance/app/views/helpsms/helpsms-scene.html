Sending SMS Messages
Message:
Enter your message (up to 160 characters) and tap send.
What happens next?
This application will log in to your Google Voice™ communications service page over the web (in the background). It will then move to your inbox, instruct Google™ that you wish to send a text message and then pass this message to Google Voice™ communications service for transmission.
Please note:
This operation may take a few moments due to possible response times from Google™. Please be patient.
