User Preferences
Google Voice™ Settings
Number:
This is the number you received when you signed up. This is required in order to make Google Voice™ communications service phone calls.
PIN:
This is the PIN number you use to log in (if you have selected to use one). P2Labs highly recommends you take advantage of this security precaution.
Google™ Account Settings
Email Address: This is the email address you use to log in to your Google™ account and is needed to send SMS text messages.
Password: This is the password you use to log in to you Google™ account and is needed to send SMS text messages.
Palm™ Phone Settings
Delay between tones: You can adjust the time between tones sent when you place your Google Voice™ communications service phone call (default is 2 seconds).
User Interface Settings
Show alerts:
You may choose to have small banner notifications appear at the base of your screen.
Use sound:
You may choose to have a small feedback sound play when you tap on commands.
Shake to go back:
Choose the shake option to enable you to shake your device and return to the previous screen.
