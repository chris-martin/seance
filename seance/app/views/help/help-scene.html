Instructions
Placing Phone Calls

Using Address Book:
Tap the Address Book icon to display & locate a contact from your address book. You will be able to place a phone call to this contact.

Using Keypad:
Tap the Keypad icon to manually enter a number. You will be able to place a phone call to this contact.
Sending Text Messages

Using Address Book:
Tap the Address Book icon to display & locate a contact from your address book. You will be able to send a text message to this contact.

Using Keypad:
Tap the Keypad icon to manually enter a number. You will be able to send a text message to this contact.
Goog-411
Call Goog-411:
Tap the Goog-411 icon to place a call to 1.800.GOOG.411. Your Palm™ phone will be launched with all the numbers. Just tap to call.
History
View Events:
Tap the History icon to display a list of calls and messages sent. You will be able to initiate a call or text message from these entries.
VoiceMail
Voicemail:
Tap the Voicemail icon to place a call to Google Voice™ and retrieve any voicemail messages you may have. Your Palm™ phone will be launched with all the numbers. Just tap to call.
